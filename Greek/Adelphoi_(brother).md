According to context, *adelphoi* from the 'same' *delphys* can mean the following:
- Physical Brothers
- Physical Brothers and Sisters
- Figurative Brothers
- Figurative Brothers and Sisters

*Adelphoi* can mean more than a blood brother
- Kinsman
    - Genesis 29:12
    - Romans 9:3
- Neighbor
    - Matthew 5:22-23
- Step-Brother
    - Mark 6:17-18
